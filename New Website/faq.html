Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unavigate and How does it work?

Unavigate is a platform where high school students can navigate their future by talking to our college advisors, signing up for internship opportunities, and preparing for the college application process. Unavigate works by pairing high school students with the college advisors they would like to talk to in order to gain insight on their respective colleges. To speak to our advisors, high schoolers will have to utilize our app (launching August).
