Our Story

Here at Unavigate we recognize that many students face an uneven playing field when it comes to college admissions. By creating a messaging service that directly connects the student to college advisors across the country, we not only cut out the middleman, but we also eliminate unnecessary fees that come with building a strong college application. From college tours to interviews with the admissions office, students are spending thousands of dollars in airfare, hotel fees, and transportation when the same information could be delivered seamlessly online.
